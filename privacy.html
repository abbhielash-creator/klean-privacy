KLEAN – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30 October 2025

1. Introduction

KLEAN (“the App”) is a cleaning service booking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with Huawei AppGallery policies and relevant data protection laws.

This app is also submitted under the Mobile APP COMPETITION UMPSA x HUAWEI APP GALLERY 2025.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information when you use the app:

  • Name, email, phone number, location (for service booking)
  • Booking details (date, time, cleaner selected)
  • Profile photo (for cleaners only)
  • Analytics and crash logs (non-identifiable device info)

Camera Permission (Profile Photo & HMS Scan)

The App may request camera access for two purposes:

  • Cleaner Profile Photo Upload: Allows taking/uploading a photo for cleaner profiles. These images are stored securely in Firebase Storage and used only inside the app.
  • HMS Scan / QR Scanning: Used to scan QR codes. Images captured during scanning are processed locally and NOT stored.

The app does not access photos unless you intentionally upload them.

Wallet Feature (Demo Only)

The wallet balance inside the app is a virtual demo feature. No real money or financial transactions occur.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process booking requests
  • Match you with cleaners based on preferences
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication, Firestore Database, and Firebase Storage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Limited data (name & booking details) may be shared with cleaners solely to perform the service.
